Get Started

Web2Video can be used to generate videos from web pages. Visit the site to sign up.

Once you have confirmed your account, you can visit your dashboard. You should generate an API key authenticate your YouTube account then you can queue tasks that produce videos.

Creating Your First Video

  • Click the Create new video button.
  • Select the first item in the Upload to dropdown.
    (this is the ID from YouTube representing your account)
  • In the URL box, copy and paste the link below:
    http://w2v.github.io/examples/hello_world
  • Leave all other options at their default and click Create Task
  • Your video will be created soon!

Adapting Examples

We have a range of examples that we're adding to regularly.

If you would like to use our examples as a base, simply copy the HTML source into a fresh file, edit it and upload it.
